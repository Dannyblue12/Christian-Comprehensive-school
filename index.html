About Us

Christian Comprehensive High School, IdongUsiong, in Akpautong, Esit Eket Local Government Area of Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria came as a vision to Late Albert Eyo Udo, an ex-commissioner for Agriculture/Natural Resources and Rural Development during the Administration of Governor Obong Akpan Isemin.

His tenure as a commissioner was felt across the state. The spread of fertilizer to farmers was his greatest contribution to boost food production.

When he was in then Cross River State, in the Ministry of Education (Technical Division) in charge of Introductory Technology in all the Secondary School in the state, his impact was felt. Workshops and seminars were conducted to sensitize the teachers and students on the use of tools and equipment.

Founding Fathers of Christian Comprehensive High School

Founder & Visionary

Late Elder Albert Eyo Udo

The visionary founder of Christian Comprehensive High School, Late Elder Albert Eyo Udo was an ex-commissioner for Agriculture/Natural Resources and Rural Development during the Administration of Governor Obong Akpan Isemin.

His tenure as a commissioner was felt across the state, with the spread of fertilizer to farmers being his greatest contribution to boost food production. His dedication to education and development laid the foundation for this institution.

Late Mr. Victor Albert Udo

Born 3rd April, 1963, Late Mr. Victor Albert Udo was the son of the founder and a firm believer in education as the cornerstone of development. He became the Principal of Christian Comprehensive High School established by his late father.

He subsequently assumed the position of Proprietor of the School and later joined the Akwa Ibom State Agricultural Development Programme (AKADEP) under the State Civil Service, rising to the rank of Principal Education Officer.
